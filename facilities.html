About Our Department

Leading the Way in Mining Education

The Department of Mining Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, established in 1956, is the first of its kind in India and continues to be a pioneer in mining education and research. Our department has consistently maintained its position as the top-ranked mining engineering program in the country.

We offer comprehensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students to become leaders in the mining industry.

Our distinguished faculty members are internationally recognized experts in their fields, actively engaged in cutting-edge research and industry collaborations.

Our World-Class Facilities

Advanced Rock Mechanics Laboratory

Equipped with state-of-the-art testing machines including MTS servo-controlled systems, triaxial testing apparatus, and acoustic emission monitoring systems for comprehensive rock property analysis.

Research Graduate Studies

Mine Ventilation & Environmental Engineering Lab

Features a fully operational mine ventilation network simulator, gas detection systems, and computational fluid dynamics software for studying air flow patterns and pollutant dispersion in mines.

Ventilation Environment

Mineral Processing & Beneficiation Center

Houses advanced equipment for comminution, classification, gravity separation, flotation, and magnetic separation processes, along with analytical instruments for mineral characterization.

Processing Beneficiation

Mine Planning & Simulation Laboratory

Equipped with high-end workstations running industry-standard software like SURPAC, DATAMINE, and VULCAN for mine design, planning, and 3D visualization of mining operations.

Software 3D Modeling

Drone & Remote Sensing Technology Center

Features advanced UAV systems with LiDAR and multispectral sensors for mine surveying, volumetric calculations, and environmental monitoring applications.

Drones Remote Sensing

Mine Safety & Disaster Management Center

Includes a virtual reality mine safety training system, gas explosion simulation setup, and emergency response equipment for comprehensive safety education and research.

Safety Disaster Mgmt.
