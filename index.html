About the Department

The Department of Mining Engineering at IIT Kharagpur is one of the oldest and most prestigious mining departments in India, with a rich legacy of academic excellence and industry contribution.

Legacy of Innovation

Established in 1956, our department has been at the forefront of mining education and research in India. We combine traditional mining knowledge with modern technological advancements to address contemporary challenges in mineral resource extraction.

Comprehensive Programs

Offering B.Tech, M.Tech, M.Sc, and Ph.D. programs with specialized electives
